About

Follow The Word was founded with the mission to bring more hearts to Christ and strengthen believers through daily encouragement and teaching. I have stuggled myself with following the path of the lord. I have found that with a reminder of his love everyday really helps me seek him in my everyday life. I want it to make a mission for people who know christ to spread the gospel to others. I feel like everyone should be able to find eternal love, but its our mission to guide them. Wether its a close friend, family member, acquaintance, or even a stranger. Just a simple "God loves you" can change someones life.
